Electric Vehicle Dashboard – Power BI Project

Overview

The Electric Vehicle Dashboard is a Power BI project designed to analyze electric vehicle trends, sales performance, adoption rates, and market insights through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

• Market trend visualization

• Trend tracking and insights
